Diploma in Anaesthesia Technology

Diploma in Anaesthesia Technology is a course dealing with anaesthesiology that is the state of unconsciousness where surgeries and other medical procedures are performed. is a two years long diploma course in the health care and medical field. This course constitutes of four semesters and also includes an internship period. Diploma in Anaesthesia Technology is a course that offers a strong foundation for students interested in medicine and human system.

Eligibility

  • Students should have either finished 12th (PUC) equivalence with physics, chemistry and biology subjects, or a diploma in paramedical sciences or allied medical sciences.
  • Applying students must have at least 45 per cent marks in their 12th standard or diploma degree.

Scope

Diploma in Anesthesia Technology is one of the most sought after career oriented course with several job opportunities in the health and patient care industry. The completion of this course can lead to a jobs as anaesthesia technologist, operation theatre anaesthesiologist, associate consultant, clinical assistant, technical assistant, medical consultant, anaesthesia consultant, professors, associate lectures etc. in government hospitals, private hospitals, universities and colleges, private clinics, health care centres etc. The fresh graduates can also go for higher education in India and abroad.
