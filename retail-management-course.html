Course

MBA in Retail Management First of all you have to understand the meaning of Retail Management, Retail means the sale of goods, services, and satisfaction to consumers. Management means the proper utilization of resources. Retail management is an integrated part of supply chain management. 7-8 years ago, Retail has been just selling goods in the market place, stores, door to door delivery, and by hand delivery. But now, the retail industries are establishing network marketing through online shopping, electronic payments, and delivery through the courier and other services. In other words, Retail management means that it is a subject that requires you to be fully aware of brands, marketing strategies, and retail philosophies winning the costumers.

MBA in Retail management is 2 year course which is separated into 4 semesters and brief description of each semester is given below

The first semester In the first semester, students learn about the basics of accounting, communication, economic analysis, behavioral sciences, and fundamentals of retails.

The second semester In this semester, students generally learn about marketing research, merchandising Management, shoppers behavior, human resource management, financial management and legal aspects of management should also be covered.

The third semester- In this semester, students got subjects like strategic marketing, franchising in retail, lean six sigma in retail, mail dynamics, and real estate management, and retail branding, and customer relationship management (CRM).

The fourth semester In the fourth and last semester, students covers subjects like advertising and sales promotion, rural retail, e-retailing, business communication, and retailing of insurance products and financial services. A project or dissertation may be required at the end of the course.
