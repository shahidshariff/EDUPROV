Bachelor of Nursing

The career of a person is decided by an undergraduate degree, he or she chooses. B.Sc Nursing is different from another bachelor's degree. Nursing is not only a degree but also a service. This course attracts lots and lots of girl students who are highly dedicated.

In any Nursing stream, the Bachelor of Nursing (B. Sc Nursing) is an undergraduate course. The main aim of the course is to show the ability in knowledge and skill in giving complete nursing care based problems with solving approaches in the hospital and community. In this way, you can do a lot for society and lots of sector needs these graduates. This profession has a great demand and it will increase in the future as well. In India, this course is registered and under controlled by Indian Nursing Council. You may be confused about how to choose the institution. This part is in our hands!! EDUPROV will help you in this case. We will guide you to choose the best institution. We understand your requirements and will recommend the institutions accordingly.

A person who is interested in serving their service to society via caring and treating patients should not think more before joining this course. If you are a nurse then you can make a huge difference in society and people’s lives. A caring and concerned nurse is considered as a guardian angel for the patients. For compassionate, it is a very satisfying and gratifying career.

A career in nursing is available for both males and females in India. There are two types of courses are available in B. Sc Nursing such as B. Sc Nursing (Basic) and B. Sc Nursing (Post Basic). The basic nursing course duration is 4 years.

Eligibility

  • The basic requirement for Nursing is 10+2 in Biology, Chemistry, and Physics.
  • Minimum and maximum age should be required 17 years and 35 years respectively. Dedication and determination alone are required. No more requirements needed.

Scope

Nursing is a recession-proof career and always demanding. Nursing staff can find a job easily and get employment in private or government-run hospitals, orphanages, nursing homes, old age homes, armed forces, railway, clinic and health departments, industries and sanatoriums. They can also find a job in the Indian Nursing Council, Red Cross Society, State Nursing Councils and many other institutes of nursing.

They can get a job of teaching and administrative positions in nursing schools and medical colleges. Enterprising people can begin their own nursing bureaus and work on their own terms and conditions. If you want to go abroad there are tremendous job prospects with a profitable salary like the USA, Singapore, Canada, UK, Gulf countries where the demand for Indian nurses is high.

Apart, from these countless openings in the domestic sector, a nurse who has an international nursing certificate and completes all the prescribed immigration criteria usually moves to greener pastures abroad. B.Sc Nursing is a great career option for future study in this stream like M.Sc (Nursing), M.Phil or Ph.D.
