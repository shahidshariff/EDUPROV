Master of Designing

Course

The esteemed National Institute of Design offers two and a half year or 5 semester Master of design courses in various disciplines. These courses are aimed at students who are passionate about design, fine arts, and contemporary thinking. The master’s course equips students to join the industry with relevant experience and expertise since they get the best design education from national and international faculty.

  • National Institute of Design, Ahmedabad, Gujarat
  • National Institute of Design, PG Campus, Gujarat
  • National Institute of Design Research and development campus, Bangalore, Karnataka

Eligibility

Candidates from the general unreserved and general/ unreserved EWS category need to be under the age of 30. Candidates belonging to PwD category need to be under the age of 35 and those belonging to ST, SC and OBS should be under the age of 33

  • Candidates in the final year or those who have completed the course can join
  • There is a total of 10 percent seats in each discipline that is reserved for OCI and NRI students
  • Candidates must have completed one of the following educational qualifications:
  • 4-year bachelor’s degree in any specialization after class 12 or equivalent education
  • 3-year bachelor’s degree in any specialization after class 12
  • PEARL Entrance Test
  • SOFT Entrance Test
  • Full-time diploma or bachelor’s degree that is for a minimum of four years in design, applied arts, architecture, fine arts, etc. after class 12
