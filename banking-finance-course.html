MBA Banking and Finance

MBA in Finance and insurance is a postgraduate course that can be achieved by students of any stream. This is a two-year program that will cover all the basics and concepts in the sector of finance and insurance. This MBA degree will also cover the basics of management along with specialized training in subjects like insurance, international banking, risk management, project and infrastructure, investment banking, and much more. The two-year course will consist of four semesters out of which two are the same for all the MBA courses. The last two semesters will provide you a specialization in your course and will cover topics related to finance and insurance.

Eligibility

The person can opt for a part-time/full-time course as per their convenience. Some universities conduct entrance examination for admission. Several national level exams such as GATE, CAT, GMAT, etc.. will be conducted for admission in MBA. Candidate should have at least 50% of graduation marks from any recognized institution are eligible. The courses are available in different modes like full time, part-time and distance learning. One can choose the mode as per their convenience. Each course has different eligibility criteria. The institution plays a major role in the career of the candidate. Therefore the wise choice is required in case of selecting the institution.

Scope

This course has a very good demand in international as well as national market. The course will make you an expert in the field of finance and insurance and you can find jobs in any local as well as international finance or insurance agencies. The applicant needs to have good self-confidence, outward skills and should be able to communicate with people different levels. One who has this degree can become an Assistant controller, Customer service representative, Internal Business analyst, Equity research analyst, Financial analyst auditor, Operations Manager, Marketing Manager, Stock analyst, Treasurer, etc.. The employment areas for this course include sectors like Banking and finance, Insurance companies, Education and teaching. Research and higher educational opportunities are really high in this technologically growing sector.
