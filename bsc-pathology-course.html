B. Sc Pathology or Bachelor of Pathology

Course

The Bachelor of Pathology course is an undergraduate program, in which you to understand disease from a tissue, cellular, molecular, biological, immunological, and functional perspective. In pathology, knowledge will be integrated from the range of science disciplines from Molecular Genetics to Human Biology. The one who chooses this course will understand the human body well. One who loves to know about the living being and what is happening in the human body or animals can opt for this course. This course will provide a different experience and wider knowledge.

The Pathology course is specially designed for those students who want to study tissues and human organs in order to identify the disease. Most of the course programs merge hands-on laboratory experience with classroom lectures. Examples of classes were undergraduates may have an opportunity to take include microbiology, cell biology, biochemistry, toxicology, and histology.

Different types of animal research included in many programs for helping students in learning. With bachelor's degree students prepared with pathology background, then after students can choose the area of specializing such as environmental pathology, molecular pathology or toxicology.

The degree prepares students for a great career in a multiplicity of subjects. It requires two to five years duration for the completion of the course; this course is available in most of the colleges and universities all over the world. Further details about the course and the institutions can be obtained from the EDUPROV where the experts are waiting to assist you with their experience and expertise.

Eligibility

  • The candidate must complete the 12th examination in the science stream (physics, chemistry, biology as a compulsory subject) from a recognized board with an assured percentage, are qualified to apply for the Undergraduate course.
  • Several medical colleges and universities offer the course in the field of Pathology.
  • Most of them normally conduct at the degree and master's degree levels.
  • For the master's degree, graduations in any related field from a recognized University are set.

Scope

Job seekers have wide opportunities in their employment areas like hospitals, Health Agencies, Investigating Agencies, Agriculture Departments, Laboratories, Medical centers, Greenhouses, Colleges or Universities, Educational Institutes, Consultancies, etc. They may get jobs in these places and they can work as a Health Care Worker, Medical Lab Technician, Medical Examiner, Dermatopathologist, Forensic Technician, Mortuary Assistant, Cytotechnologist, Consultant, etc.
