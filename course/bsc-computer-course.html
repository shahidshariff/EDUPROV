B.Sc Computer Science

Course Information

B.sc Computer Science is a course about computers and computational systems. In this course, one will know about the computer and its operations (both hardware and software). The duration of this course is 3 years. This course in 3 years will make you understand the science behind the computer in detail. If you are interested to know more about the world of computers and love to know what happens in it and interested to play with it you can opt for this course.

Computer technology will provide vast knowledge and will help in every field. The course provides a wide knowledge of computer and its technology, which includes subjects such as Calculus, Operating Systems, Data Structures, Computer Organization, Programming Languages, and Software Engineering.

There are several benefits if you opt for a B.Sc course. The duration of this course is only three years. B.Sc degree holder has various options after completing their graduation, one can opt for pursuing higher studies in their core, management or even can opt for teaching careers.
