BBA

Course Information

Business administration is a challenging task. Every single decision is very important in a business which may lead to growth or loss. Every single person who loves to face adventures in day to day life in the field of business can choose this course. This course is not limited to the person who wants to start a business. The person one who wants to change the world of business can also choose this challenging course.

The Bachelor of Business Administration (B.B.A or BBA) is an undergraduate program in business administration and commerce. It is a 3 years degree course that awarded you after finishing your study in one or more areas of business attentions. In BBA program you can learn about the general business course and the advanced course also for specific attention. Further details can be obtained from the experts of EDUPROV who have years of experience in guiding the candidates.

The BBA degree is specially designed to provide a wide knowledge about the functional aspects of a company and their interconnection, while you can also allowing for specialization in a particular area. BBA program display students to a selection of “core subjects” and allow you to specialize in a particular academic area. It also develops your practical, managerial and communication skills and increases your capability of decision-making. Lots of programs incorporate practical experience and training, in the form of presentations, projects, internships, interaction and industrial visits with experts from the industry.

The course may emphasize the development of quantitative reasoning, communications, and business analysis skills. With this, you can gain knowledge of business process and practices, awareness of global business issues, business theory, business law and ethics, e-business, project management, a role of economics and entrepreneurship fundamentals.

Eligibility

The candidate should be a pass Higher Secondary or equivalent above 40%. The General age requirement is 17 years.

The candidate needs to submit an application along with all necessary documents such as the 10th and 12th mark sheet, school leaving certificate, identity proof, domicile certificate, and extracurricular certificate.

Now everything is modernized and the admission application forms can easily be downloaded from the college website or you can visit EDUPROV’s website to fill the application by selecting multiple colleges and uploading necessary documents and EDUPROV Admission Counselors will assist you in admission to your preferred college of yours.

Scope

A Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) gets you entry level positions in many factories, companies and corporations. Higher education options are open after BBA, if you continue your study then you can apply for MBA to join the corporate world in a Managerial role, MCA for technical field of commerce or Post graduation Diploma to get specialization in various fields like Accounting, Finance, Information Technology etc.

Private Sector: After BBA you need to join firms at an executive level and you preferred for positions like Finance and HR also. With experience, you will be able to rise to higher heights. It is important for you to seek a career in an area where you believe your interest extend.

Government Sector: You must need to check job opportunities notifications on a daily basis. You must visit job portals too. They have many job options according to your interest or job preferred like banking jobs. When nationalized banks need a candidate with this profile, they can use them for multitasking. There are also some other options are available in the Civil Service. You can be appearing for state level exams such as railways, PSC ( Public Service Commission )etc.

Your starting salary depends on various things such as a profile of the employer, job profile, job location, institute from which you have completed your education etc. So be careful about your institute selection because it makes your future, good institute makes your career, your quality institute plays a huge role in campus selection and determining the starting salary figure.
