Bachelor of Archaelogy

Do you like the thought of studying about historic events and items? If so, adegree in archaeology makes perfect sense. It is the perfect way to get a chance of working with historical artifacts, at historical sites, get the opportunity of working in the restoration of monuments, to explore the hidden ancient history of the world. Archaeologists get to work with ancient remains, culture, history, and traditions.This is an interdisciplinary course that involves the research of elements developed by earlier human civilizations. The prime intention of this course is to study past human civilizations through survey, description, and excavation of the ancient sites and the items found there. It is an excellent option and it leads to a wide range of job opportunities both in India and overseas.

Syllabus

During this course, students get to study Ancient history, principles and methods of archaeology, before christ, museology, a timeline of ancient history, classic antiquity, early iron age, greek and roman civilizations, structural conservation of ancient monuments, late ancient history, epigraphy and numismatics, prehistory, middle to the late bronze age, application of science in archaeology, early ancient history, chemical preservation of monuments and antiquities, history and post-classical history etc. Through this course, students can choose to specialize in geoarchaeology, Ethano Archaeology, Archaeobotany, Environmental Archaeology, Marine Archaeology, Battlefield Archaeology, Urban Archaeology, Experimental Archaeology, Archaeozoology etc.

This course is usually provided alone or with a combination of complementary majors like museology, ancient history, ancient Indian history, culture, vocational archaeology, English, Language etc.
