BMS College of Engineering

College Information

BMS College of Engineering is situated in Bangalore in Karnataka state of India. Established in 1946, it is accredited from AICTE, NAAC, NBA and it is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University. BMSCE, Bangalore offers 29 courses across 3 streams namely Engineering, Management, IT and across 6 degrees like B.Tech, B.Arch, M.Tech, MCA, MBA. BMSCE campus is spread over 15 Acres. Hostel facility is available for its students. Additional campus facilities such as Academic Zone, A/C, Auditorium, ATM, Boys Hostel, Canteen, Computer Lab, Counselling, Fest, Cultural zone are also there. 'BMS College of Engineering' accepts Karnataka PGCET - Karnataka Post Graduate Common Entrance Test for admission into Engineering courses

Admission Procedure

  • The candidates have to fill an online application form.
  • Candidates should qualify either of the entrance tests: Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET)/Consortium Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka (COMEDK).
  • Admission will be based on the performance in KCET/ COMEDK.
  • The candidates have to fill an online application form.
  • For M.Tech, admission will be given to the candidates who have a valid GATE/ PGCET score.
  • For MBA, admission will be given to the candidates who qualify any one of the entrance tests: KMAT/ CMAT/ MAT/ PGCET.
  • For MCA, admission will be given to the candidates who qualify any one of the entrance tests: KMAT/ PGCET.
  • Candidates have to fill an online registration form.
  • Admission will be given to the candidates based on letter of purpose, marks scored in 12th class, bachelor's examination and master's examination.
