Eligibility

Well, for becoming a fashion designer, there is course named Bachelor in Fashion Design and Technology. This course is one of the prolific course, as the arena of this course is quite wide. People learn about the trend, colour combination, designs, ornament design and many other things that transform a useless piece of cloth into the trendiest outfit.

  • The minimum age limit is 18 years.
  • Must have completed High school and Intermediate from any stream
  • Should be of fervent nature towards fashion study
  • Must qualify in the interview round
  • If applying outside India, then need to have either IELETS or TOFEL scores

Scope

As mentioned above, the fever of fashion will never die and the people always love to welcome new trends and fashion stuff. So, the scope of the course is very high for those who are creative in this area. People can thrive in their future as there are so many things to do.

  • You can join any textile company.
  • You can join any ornamental firm
  • You can also go assessing various big designers
  • Or you can also open your boutique and can impress others with your creative work.

You also have the chance to go overseas as there is a huge scope of the trends and mainly France is quite famous for this as the maximum fashion weeks are held over there. Apart from France, other countries like the USA, UK, and Australia also invites fresh and creative designers' minds.
