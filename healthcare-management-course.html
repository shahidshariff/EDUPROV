MBA Healthcare Management

Course

It is one of the fastest-growing fields. A career in health care management provides opportunities for those interested in an evolving career. Healthcare Management courses deal with managing the hospitals, health care centers and various others.

In this course, an aspirant learns all the basic and advance terms of management. Within the course, a person understands how to deal with the needs of a hospital while managing the staff.

Eligibility

A candidate who wishes to get a degree in healthcare management can go with any one of the following options. One can either have a bachelor’s degree or a master’s in this management course.

Bachelor’s Degree: The minimum education level to get avail in this program is to pass 10+2 in any subjects. Every college and institution have their own way of conducting entrance exam. Even the individual from the non-medical background can opt for this course. The least passing marks to get admission in this course are 50 percentages. The duration of the course has been for 3 years consisting of 6 semesters.

Master’s degree: To get avail in the Master’s degree it is necessary to complete the bachelor’s degree. Most of the colleges require that the candidates should have studied medicine. There is also a need to qualify in common entrance exams like XAT, MAT, and CAT. There are no age criteria for the completion of this course. A candidate once gets selected, then they have to study for 2 years consisting of 4 semesters. The course of the Master’s degree in Healthcare Management is different from other Management courses.

Scope

After successful completion of the course, an aspirant can work with the managing staff of a hospital. For fresher the management of a healthcare institution has a wide range of job opportunities. For starting the work of assistant suits the best. The working area can be of drug abuse treatment centers, hospitals, outpatient clinics, and hospices.

The common job profiles covering in this course are Healthcare Finance Managers, Heads of Departments, Medical Directors, Hospital Superintendents, Administrators, Nursing Directors, and Deputy Superintendents. For fresher to work aboard there are fewer opportunities. However, after gaining experience, it becomes easy for applying for jobs in foreign countries.
