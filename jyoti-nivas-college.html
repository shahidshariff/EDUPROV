Jyoti Nivas College

Jyoti Nivas College (JNC) Bangalore is the 8th Best Arts College in Bangalore as ranked by the India Today magazine. The college offers B.A, B.Sc, B.Com, BBA, and BCA through Undergraduate degrees and MBA. MCA, M.A, M.Com, and M.Sc through Postgraduate degree. Admission to these courses is both merit and Entrance based.

The popular course of the college is MBA and MCA and students placed with these degrees get an average salary package of 3 LPA. Students are placed in top reputed companies such as Goldman Sachs, J.P Morgan, MindTree, Wipro, SAP-labs, Cognizant, Infosys, etc.
