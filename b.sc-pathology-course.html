Bachelor of Pathology

Bachelor of Pathology course is an undergraduate program in which you to understand disease from a tissue, cellular, molecular, biological, immunological, and functional perspective. In pathology, knowledge will be integrated from the range of science disciplines from Molecular Genetics to Human Biology. The one who chooses this course will understand the human body well. One who loves to know about the living being and what is happening in the human body or animals can opt for this course. This course will provide a different experience and wider knowledge.

The Pathology courseis specially designed for those students who want to study tissues and human organs in order to identify the disease. Most of the course programs merge hands-on laboratory experience with classroom lectures. Examples of classes were undergraduates may have an opportunity to take include microbiology, cell biology, biochemistry, toxicology, and histology.

Different types of animal research included in many programs for helping students in learning. With bachelor's degree students prepared with pathology background, then after students can choose the area of specializing such as environmental pathology, molecular pathology or toxicology.

The degree prepares students for a great career in a multiplicity of subjects.It requires two to five years duration for the completion of the course; this course >is available in most of the colleges and universities all over the world.
