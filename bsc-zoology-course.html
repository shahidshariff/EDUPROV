BSc Zoology

Course

Zoology is a subject that everyone will have the basic idea of what it is and what it deals with. B.Sc zoology is somehow a detailed study of what you learned from your childhood about animals. This 3-year course provides some in-depth knowledge about animals. The person will learn about the complete ecology and the biodiversity of the animals. This includes both the extinct species like dinosaurs and the available species like monkeys.

The one who opts for this course will study the subjects that help them to understand about the animal kingdom in their six semesters. The subject includes life and diversity of various divisions, environmental biology, mammalian physiology, Aquaculture, and pest management and evolution and developmental biology. Apart from the theory section each and, every year has a practical section which helps to know more about the animals.

There are several benefits upon choosing this course. The availability of specializations in higher studies is comparatively high. You will feel satisfied when you help animals. This course gives you a tremendous experience of understanding the animal kingdom that may lead to goosebumps.

If you are the one who has the specified eligibility criteria and has an interest in animals then you can opt for this course. The course has a high scope and will make you different from what you are now. One major thing that a person who loves to opt for this course should know to sketch. Yes!! In the course duration, you need to sketch various figures of the animals and their organs.

Scope

After completion of this course, one has various job opportunities. The job types include zookeeper, veterinary technologist, caretaker of animals, technician, environmental consultant, a lab assistant in museums, hospitals, wildlife rehabilitation centers, Pharma companies, agricultural department, etc. or they can even work as a zoology teacher when opted for B.Ed., M.Ed., or M. Phil. Research scope if high in this course where one can choose areas such as environmental studies that have high scope in this era.

The one who opts for this course has a wider opportunity for higher studies in foreign countries. They also have vast and better job opportunities abroad.
