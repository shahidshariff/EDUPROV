Human Resource Management

As the name indicates human resource management deals with dealing the human resource and needs. Human resource management (HRM) deals mainly with the employees and their performance. HRM helps to improve the performance of the employees. The Human capital of an organization is managed by a Human resource professional. Human Resource graduates will focus on several processes such as recruitment, training, employee relations, etc. that deals with the human capital and how to make them beneficial for the organization by satisfying both the employee and organizational needs.

We are humans and upon choosing the course one will get the opportunity to help the humans. This course helps to understand the needs of every human being in a working environment. Since this course depends on humans and not on the machine the scope of this course will not diminish until human existence in work enforcement.
