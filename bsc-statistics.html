B.Sc Statistics

Course

The process of collecting, analyzing, interpreting, presenting, and organizing of data is simply known as statistics. Bachelor of Statistics is an undergraduate degree which is a 3-year course. Statistics is required in every industry and it has a wide variety of job scope in every field. The modern statistical methods require a person to have more advance knowledge in exploring data and modeling it.

To study the course a person should be having a strong mathematical background. Even if they are not having a good background, then they should be good in Mathematics. There will be many numerical values that come and different approaches to solving them. Within the course, a candidate learns how to handle complex data and come out with the required solution.

The Course of Statistics deals with the concepts of a collection of dataâ€™s in the form of numbers, alphabets or symbols. There are different courses, Bachelor of Statistics that deals in Finance, Economics, Commerce, Science and Social Science. In the course, a student learns about financial statistics, Bio statistics, sampling and database, experimental design, computational inference, inference and environmental statistics.

Eligibility

The first one includes having a minimum educational qualification of Sr. secondary (10+2) or its equivalent exam with mathematics and English subject of study compulsorily, also including a minimum of 55% marks. An important requirement is to have a good command of English language and communication.

In some institutions a special benefit is given to all the awardees of any preceding year, they need not appear in the written test as they shall be directly invited to give the interview. It is common for the institutes or colleges to conduct their own entrance test and Personal Interviews.

Scope

During the period of development or the modern age, statistics can also be called as indispensable. The governments all over the world of different countries are in continuous research for improving their economic progress. Here comes the role of statistical analysis techniques and statistical data as it is greatly helpful in solving the issues related to price, wages, demand analysis, and time series analysis. It has now become an extremely rare instrument of production control.

For studying the desires of the customers the business executives are depending more towards statistical techniques Studies in a bachelor of statistics opens the gates of a wide variety of industries. One will enjoy a promising career. Also, after completing bachelors, one can always choose to go for higher studies in statistics, mathematics, computer science and economics. After doing the course, one can make a career as a statistician in scientific laboratories and research institutions. You can also get a job in government departments or industries.

Any international company would love to have a person who is good in statistical measures. The jobs are regularly posted and you can apply to them easily. There are also companies abroad who ask to gather data, which is an easy and nice startup work.
