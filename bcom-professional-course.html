B.com Professional

Course

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com professionals) is an undergraduate degree where one will gain knowledg +e in areas like accounting, taxation, and finance in the course duration of 3 years. The program aims at developing Accountancy and Finance professionals to meet the Industry requirements in India and Abroad.

Commerce acts as the key to every nation that allows maintaining a good relationship with another nation. The business in each and every country depends on this major anchor (i.e.,) commerce. Since the dependency for this sector is high in every nation the scope of this course is also higher across the map.

Eligibility

In order to get into the course, one should have Pass in 10+2 grade or equivalent with subjects like accountancy, business mathematics, Economics, or Maths. The candidate should also require to have good proficiency in the English Language.

A student without basic knowledge of accountancy can't apply for this course. (I.e.,) a student from the science group is not eligible for this course. Further assistance and complete guidance for each and every level will be provided by the EDUPROV experts. Scope

The Bachelor of Commerce fresher can start his career in areas like Banking– Retail / personal banking, Corporate Banking, Merchant Banking, Treasury group, rural banking, Product management, Loans executive Insurance- Insurance and Risk management, Insurance surveyor, Insurance surveyor, Insurance agent Accounts– Chartered Accountant, Cost and Work Accountant, Certified Financial Analyst, Certified financial planner. One can also opt for higher Education in Management/Commerce and also in pursuing a professional qualification such as CA, ACCA, etc. Higher education likeMBA or other master’s degrees along with the experience will lead you to get further promotions in the future.
