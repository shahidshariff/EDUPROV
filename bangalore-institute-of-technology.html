Bangalore Institute of Technology

Bangalore Institute of Technology (BIT) was started in the year 1979 with the Intention of providing quality education in the field of technology and thereby serving the society. BIT is one of the best colleges affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VIT), Belgaum located in Bangalore.

The Institute has been accredited by NBA & recognized by AICTE. It is an engineering college offering undergraduate and graduate engineering courses such as BE, MBA, M.Tech etc. Bangalore Institute of Technology Admission to all courses is done through state/ national level entrance exams such as JEE Main, GATE, KMAT, CMAT.

Admission Procedure

  • Fill in the application form.
  • Duly filled form should be submitted along with two passport size photographs.
  • Fees payment can be done either by cash/ cheque on the same date.
  • This confirms the enrollment procedure. Students are personally informed about the date of commencement of their course.

Original marks card of SSLC/10Std/intermediate for, age proof, 10+2/PUC/equivalent, Original transfer certificates, Original migration certificate (for other than the Karnataka State Board students).

It is to be noted that qualified foreign nationals are provisionally admitted subject to regularization after obtaining the eligibility certificate issued by the Bangalore university. Student visa, Residential permit, and serology report (HIV Test), AIU Letter for foreign nationals are a must.
