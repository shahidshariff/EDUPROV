Bachelor of Unani Medicine & Surgery

Course

Unani System of Medicine & Surgery (BUMS) is also known as Kamil-e-Tib-o-Jarahat and is one of the oldest medication systems in the world. UnaniSystem originated in Greece and from there the system of medication was adopted by Middle-East and South-Asian countries. It is a program under the AYUSH category – Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy – it is monitored by the Central Council of Indian Medicine. It is a 4 ½-year undergraduate degree that deals with the medical subjects and applications of the Unani system and surgery. Unani is a 6,000-year-old medical system of medicine that is different from regular medications (allopathy). In this, the healing process is done through the natural processes/ natural power of the body. As per the Unani System, the body has the power to heal itself. Unani focuses on four different treatment therapies in its field: massage, diuresis, Turkish bath, pharmaco-therapy, diet therapy, purging, regimental therapy venesection, exercise; use of herbal, animal, and mineral drugs, and surgery. All that the human needs to do is identify the healing power within themselves and enhance it. It is known for treating various deadly diseases and disorders in a natural way as every man in the modern age is inflicted with stress and anxieties which is a root cause of various diseases.

Eligibility

  • The minimum qualification period is 12th or PUC education with Physics, chemistry, and biology as the core subjects
  • Students need 50 percent. Another way to have the necessary educational background is to pass the Pre-Tib one-year exam.
  • The student must also be at least 17 years of age at the time of admission for the BMS course. For some institutions, the applicant must also submit a certificate that he or she has passed the 8th Class Examination with Hindi as well.
  • The admissions mainly take place by the state CET exams or through the application to state CET exams with he/she NEET score if Student want to get admissions for medicine through government quota
  • Student can also apply for the COMED-K exam for the seats through COMED-K. Several top private colleges for Unani have admissions through management quota.

Scope

There are many employment opportunities available for B.U.M.S students after completion of their degree both in the public and private sectors. After graduating, students can either decide to work or continue higher education by doing a M.D in Unani from top medical colleges in Bangalore and top medical colleges in India. Graduates can even start a private practice or self-practice as hakims. Unani pharmaceutical and pharmacies are also hiring B.U.M.S. graduates as there is a huge dearth of professionals in the Unani sector.
