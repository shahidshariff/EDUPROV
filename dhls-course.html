Diploma in Audiology and Speech therapy

Course

Diploma in audiology and speech therapy is generally a one or two years long course that is designed to train people to cater to the needs of individuals who suffer from communication disorders right from the grass root levels. Students receive training that will help them easily identify communication disorders and help in further diagnosis and treatment by making the initial reference and identification of the disorder. The Diploma in audiology and speech therapy course contains a theoretical component and practical component that will help the students obtain both theoretical and practical knowledge. Students will be learning in detail about audiology, speech-language pathology, language pathology, psychology, community-based rehabilitation, special education, clinical psychology, etc.

Eligibility

Generally, there is no entrance exam for this course but, some of the top government institutes of India have their own entrance exam. Apart from that, the basic admission eligibility to join the diploma in audiology and speech therapy course are as follows-

  • Admission to Diploma in audiology and Speech therapy is open to candidates who have passed 12th standard / II PUC with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
  • Students who have finished the 10th standard and a diploma in any paramedical field can also apply for the diploma in audiology and speech therapy.
  • Candidates should have obtained a minimum of 50% of the aggregate in the qualifying examination. The minimum aggregate score for SC and ST candidates is 45%.
  • Candidates should also have working knowledge in the English language. They must have taken up English as a subject in their 11th and 12th standard examinations.
