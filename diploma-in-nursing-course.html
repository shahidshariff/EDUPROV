Diploma in Nursing care Technology

Course

Diploma in Nursing care technology is a three-year-long Diploma Program that covers Nursing Care, Technology, and Administration. Nursing care related in taking care of patient hygiene, serving meals to patients, administering medications and arranging equipment in the patient room. Students taking up Diploma in nursing will be learning Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Microbiology, Psychology, Sociology, Fundamentals of Nursing, Personal and Patient Hygiene, First Aid and Care, Community Health, Nutrition, Environmental Hygiene, Health Education, Communication Skills, Pharmacology, Specialities, Mental Health and Psychiatric Nursing, Gynaecology, Midwifery, Paediatric Nursing etc.

Eligibility

The basic eligibility for the Diploma in Nursing Care Technology is that the Students should complete their 10+2 examination with biology, Physics, Chemistry, and English with an aggregate of 50 Percent Marks and above from a Recognized board
