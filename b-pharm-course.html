B Pharmacy

Course

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B Pharm) is a four-year undergraduate program in which students study the methods and process of preparing drugs and how does the dispensing of medicine and drugs take place. Pharmacy is an excellent choice of course for students interested in medicine, the making, production, composition, and medical research or pharmaceutical research. There are several courses in science stream and medical stream but Bachelor of Pharmacy or B Pharma as it is commonly known as an extremely opportunistic course for students who are focused in the sciences and like the direct aspect of change it brings.

This course witnesses all the information about pharmacy or the medicines and do teach the students about its consequences on the human body. Under this course, the students get the precise overture of ample of the medicines.

Eligibility

Apply for the state CET exam. Example, Karnataka students shall apply for KCET.Attempt the exam for PCB or PCM or PCMB subjects depending on the subjects you took in 11th and 12th.

  • The ranks formed will be on the basis of your best PCM or PCB marks.
  • Decide which college is the best for your ranking and select it during the counseling rounds held by KCET
  • If opting for this course at the undergraduate level, one needs to have the passing certificate of High school and Intermediate, 10+2 or PUC.
  • If opting for this course at the post-graduate level, one needs to have an aggregate of 50% marks in biology at the undergraduate level from a recognized university.
  • The minimum age required is 17 years old.

The student must have had either physics, chemistry and mathematics or physics, chemistry and biology subjects in 11th and 12th. This means that this course is for both PCM and PCB students stream from a recognized board, with an aggregate of 50%.

Students wishing to get a seat in B pharmacy through government quota seats must compulsorily give CET exams of their respective courses. But, there are other private college and university entrance exams for B Pharma. Read along to find out more about the procedures and different entrance exams for B Pharmacy course. Even direct admissions or management quota admissions are also possible in few colleges.
