Bachelor of Dental Surgery

BDS Admission which is an undergraduate level degree program in the field of oral health care. Candidates who wish to groom their careers in dental surgery can opt for this course. Science stream of biology group are eligible to pursue this professional course after their 12th. BDS course focuses mainly on the field of dental science related to oral issues. It is a popular undergraduate professional course after MBBS which on completion leads to a BDS degree and title of a doctor (Dr) In India it is the second most popular medical course dangling MBBS. This most favoured course trains students to become proficient in their dental practices independently or under supervision of a hospital.

In general Dentistry is a branch of medicine that deals with purely with the study, diagnosis, prevention of dental problems. This BDS degree is the degree given to a person who plays a pivotal role in the treatment of problems related to teeth. Dentist prevents diagnoses and cures to all dental and related diseases. Dentists are a meaningful part of educating every single person on oral health habits. They convey the importance of brushing, flossing, prevention of cavities, healthy eating habits and many such activities which we are unaware and which affects our oral health. There are many reasons to be a dentist as there are many dentists and this is a career filled with many obstacles and challenges but is also with many rewards it gives an opportunity to help people and promote the betterment of community health. Dentists shape the future of oral health care by enrolling themselves in dental education and research. A large part of dentistry involves restoring teeth and giving patients a beautiful smile. The curriculum of BDS is being regulated by the Dental Council of India (DCI). With the intention of getting admission in BDS course it is mandatory for students to appear and qualify in the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET). There is no other exam conducted out of which they can opt for BDS because NEET is the only getaway

Eligibility

  • Students from science stream biology group (PCB) students who have passed their 12th boards with a minimum score of 50% marks in PCB subjects. In the case of reservation, the minimum score drops to 45 %.
  • The lower age limit for BDS admission and the entrance exam is 17 years while the upper age limit is 25 years.
  • One should have qualifying marks in entrance exams National level tests like NEET.
  • The rank obtained in this exam determines the selection of students for pursuing a medical degree.
  • There are no restrictions on the maximum NEET attempt limit.
