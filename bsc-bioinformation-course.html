B.Sc Bioinformatics

B.Sc Bioinformatics or Bachelor of Science in Bioinformatics is a three-year undergraduate degree. Bioinformatics is a course of bioscience that applies statistics to molecular biology. Bioinformatics helps to understand the biological data with the help of the software tools. The main purpose of this course is to increase the understanding of biological processes. It focuses on developing and applying intensive techniques such as pattern recognition, data mining, machine learning algorithms, and visualization.

The subjects that one will learn includes Programming in C, Cell Biology, Genetics, Essential Mathematics and Statistics, Bioinformatics, Data Structure and Algorithms, Microbiology, Biotechnology, Biochemistry, Immunology etc.

This course is suitable for those whose interest is to invent new things. This course has a wide research opportunity. If you enjoy doing research and like to know about the latest technological advancement of science, then this course will suit you perfectly. After completing this degree one can opt for management studies, higher studies in the core area or can pursue the B. Ed degree as per the person's personal interest and convenience.

Scope

The scope for Bioinformatics is good and wide. Most of the professionals are employed in fields such as biotechnology, biomedical sciences, and pharmaceutical companies. Graduates can work in the Government and Private Hospitals. Candidates can also opt for education in universities and colleges. Some top companies are hiring professionals in Bioinformatics such as Wipro, Reliance, Tata Consultancy Services, IBM Life Sciences, etc.

Students can go abroad for their higher studies. Bioinformatics graduates are always in good demand in Western Countries and Europe. Professionals who worked in Pharmaceuticals, Biotechnology, and Energy, etc. can easily get a job in abroad.
