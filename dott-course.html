Diploma in Operation Theatre Technology

Diploma in Operation Theatre Technology is a wonderful course that students who wish to be in the Health Care Services and Medical Industry can take up. This is a Diploma Course that can be taken up by students after the completion of either 12th or PUC, a Diploma Course or even right after 10th standard. This is a Paramedical Course with very high Demand in the Health Care Industry because Operation Theatre Technology is a very important role which ensures that patients receive care and attention in the operation theatre during surgeries. It prepares the students to get training on assisting in Diagnosis, Treatment and also learn how to handle lab equipment, perform lab experiments to determine test results etc. The course is for two years and it includes an extra six months of internships for the students so that they can gain Practical experience and gain exposure in the field of Operation Theatre Technology

Eligibility

  • Students need to have either finished 12th (PUC) equivalence with physics, chemistry and biology subjects, or a diploma in paramedical sciences or allied medical sciences after 10th
  • Applying students must have at least 45 percent marks. The application for the diploma in operation theatre technology can be completed and submitted online on the college website or applied manually. You will have to attend the counselling to get a seat in the diploma in operation theatre technology course

Scope

Graduates can choose to go for Higher Education or take up jobs after the Diploma in Operation Theatre Technology (DOTT). Some of the popular jobs for Graduates of DOTT course are Operation Theatre Technician, Operation Theatre Assistant, Lab Technician, Lab Assistant, Research Assistants, etc. in doctor's Clinics, Government Hospitals, private Hospitals, Clinics, Nursing Homes, Medical writing centers or Companies, Medical Colleges, and Universities.
