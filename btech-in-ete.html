B.Tech Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering

Course

B.Tech Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering (ETE) branch of engineering deals with signal and data communication systems with the help of various transmission mediums from the origin of the signal and its destination. This branch of engineering will help one to develop devices that can be used in computers like, transistors, PCBs and integrated circuits, mobiles, MP3 players, etc. This course deals with electronic devices and software interfaces.

In this field, the engineer's work involves the strategic mass developments from the basic circuit design. The basic objects of electronics and telecommunication engineering are to develop high depth of expertise and knowledge of communication engineering, boost the speed of technical process and innovations, and make technology simple and understandable for all.

Telecommunication engineers are the experts of electronics and electrical who may specialize in certain technologies like telephone networks, broadband telecommunications, signal processing, wireless communication, optical communication and more.

Eligibility

  • The Basic requirement is a qualified Higher Secondary Exam or 10+2 with physics, chemistry, and mathematics as the main subject.
  • On the basis of HSC and entrance exam marks merit list declared.

The Candidate should appear in the entrance exam on state and all India levels like Common Entrance Test (CET), IIT JEE (Joint Entrance Examination), AIEEE (All India Engineering Entrance Examination).

Scope

It is centralized on electrical and computer engineering that facilitates to enhance and support telecommunication systems. The telecommunication engineers are hired by the companies and industries which work on the principle of wired and wireless telecommunication systems, government organizations, and other engineering-based consultancy firms. Various job opportunities are available in public and private sectors.

Lots of government organizations and private companies are available in the market that hires engineers such as Airtel, Idea, Reliance, MTNL, BSNL, Vodafone, Motorola, DRDO, ISRO, BHEL, Wipro, Intel, HCL, etc. Huge job opportunities are available for Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers; they can work as a Broadcast engineer after finishing the degree in telecommunication.
