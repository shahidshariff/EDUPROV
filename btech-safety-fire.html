B.Tech Fire & Safety Engineering

B.Tech Safety and Fire Engineering is a life-saving course in the field of engineering. Within the course, an aspirant will understand how to tackle conditions in an exclusive time. The course teaches how to take precautions and make an instant decision when the problem occurs. It refers to the precautions against the fire and others to access the safe. This education is not provided by a large number of colleges or institutions in India. There are few colleges or institutions which are providing the various courses in the field of fire and safety. It seeks to provide a fool-proof method of designing buildings and facilities for life safety and property protection in the event of an unwanted fire. It is a very dangerous profession that requires a strong sense of commitment to public service.

Scope

The candidate having the skills is liable to work in dangerous areas involving heavy fire. The scope of the job is in government and private sectors. There are much higher chances of getting hired by the manufacturing plant, multinational industries, Rigs plant, refinery, the airline industry and few more. A degree holder is also having the ultimate chances of working with abroad companies. Safety precautions are now mandatory in all establishments. There is plenty of jobs in fire and safety field. Safety is an integral part of our daily life.
