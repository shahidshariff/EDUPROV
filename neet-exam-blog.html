NEET Exam

NEET also called as National Eligibility cum Entrance Test is an all India pre medical entrance test for students who are willing to pursue any sort of Medical, Dental, Veterinary course and AYUSH courses pertaining to Ayurveda and siddha medicine.

NEET is the only gateway for students to get into the top private and government institutions. Also for those who want to pursue any medical or dental related courses abroad.

This exam is been conducted by the NTA also called as National Testing Agency which provides the all over India results to the Directorate General of Health Services under Ministry of Health and Family welfare and state counseling authorities for seat allocation.

Previously it was AIPMT All India Pre Medical Test conducted by various states and various medical colleges. Big private institutions like AIIMS All India Institute of Medical Sciences and JIPMER Jawaharlal Institute of Post Graduate Medical Education and Research conducted their own entrance exams until NEET UG became a common entrance test after the enactment of NMC Act of September 2019. Now NEET is the only entrance exam which will open your doors to get into top medical colleges if wishing to pursue medical.

Eligibility

  • candidates must qualify their 12th examination with chemistry, biology, physics and English as main subjects.
  • It is mandatory for General category students to score 50% marks in 12th boards.
  • Whereas the SC/ST category must score 40% marks for eligibility in NEET.
  • Citizens of India
  • Non Resident Indians (NRI`s)
  • Persons of Indian Origin (PIO`s)
  • Other Citizens of India (OCI`s) and foreign nationals are eligible to appear for NEET exam.

Candidates should have completed 17 years of age as of December 31st. Any decision on the maximum upper limit of age is still pending thus all candidates are provisionally eligible to apply for NEET.

As with the amendments of 2018, the restrictions on the number of attempts for NEET exam was removed. So as per the new rules there is no limit towards appearance of the exam. A candidate will be allowed to appear for the exam as many times that he could attempt only if they are eligible.

NEET UG EXAM PATTERN

  • The exam will be conducted in offline mode i.e. pen and paper based mode.
  • There will multiple types of questions in exam.
  • Candidates will have to fill their responses online.
  • The question paper will be divided into 3 sections. Physics, chemistry and biology.
  • The duration of the exam is 3 hours.
  • There will be one negative marking for a wrong response.
  • For every correct response four marks will be awarded.
  • The NEET exam question paper will be available in 11 different languages.
  • Gujarati, Assamese, Hindi, Tamil, English, Marathi, Bengali, Urdu, Oriya, Kannada, and Telugu.
  • The candidates can choose the language according to their medium of instruction.
