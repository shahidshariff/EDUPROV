Bachelor of Science (B.Sc)

Course

Science can be seen in day to day life. The universe is built up by science. Science can be experienced by everyone right from childhood. Science is an adventurous course with lots of experiments.

Bachelor of Science (B. Sc) is globally awarded at universities for the undergraduate course in Science or technology and their related fields. It is a 3-year degree program and its core subjects are Biology, Botany, Chemistry, Biochemistry, Computer Science, Environmental Science, Mathematics, Electronics, Physics, and Zoology.

Biological and life science, natural science, engineering, and technology are the key fields of the academic study of B. Sc degree course program.

Now, various B. Sc programs are running. So you have many options to choose your career field. The most popular Bachelor of Science degrees is B.sc in Computer Science, Engineering, Business, IT, Biology, Biotechnology, Applied Computer Science, Psychology, Environmental Science, Business Management, Finance, Chemistry, Information Systems, Computer Engineering, Mathematics, etc.

Eligibility

The basic requirement is qualified Higher Secondary Examination (10+2) above 50% marks with recognized Education Board in Science stream. Generally, the percentage can be varied according to state and institute. Some Quality Institutes conduct entrance exam, you need to qualify for getting admission.

Scope

The Bachelor of Science (B. Sc) graduates have a wide scope of work. It includes Chemist, Biology Researcher, Doctor, Dairy Technologist, Geneticist, Laboratory Technician, Lecturer, Oceanographers, Marine Geologists, Science Adviser, Plant Biochemist, Toxicologist, Taxonomist, etc.

The employment area of B. Sc is also wide, they can get work in Biotechnology Firms, Chemical Industry, Agriculture Industry, Educational Institutes, Environment Management, and Conservation, Forensic Research, Food Institutes, Forest Services, Geological Survey Departments, Health Care Providers, Hospitals, Industrial Laboratories, Oil Industry, Pharmaceuticals and Biotechnology Industry, Research Firms, Seed and Nursery Companies, Space Research Institutes, Testing Laboratories, Wildlife and Fishery Departments, Waste Water Plants and more.

B. Sc graduates are not limited in just science-based fields they can also work in various non-scientific fields also. They can also find jobs in technical writing, marketing, business, banking sector, etc. A candidate who continues higher studies after B.Sc they have an opportunity to becomes a Scientist in Biotic research, Lecturer, Technologist, etc.
