MBA Logistic Management

Course

Logistics Management is part of the supply chain function. In general, logistics is the management of the flow of things between the point of origin and the point of consumption in order to meet customer’s requirements.

One who opts for Logistics management will gain knowledge in areas such as fleet management, inbound and outbound transportation management, warehousing, materials handling, order fulfillment, logistics network design, inventory management, supply/demand planning, and management of third-party logistics service providers.

Logistics Management is important for every organization. As the business develops so do the scope of logistics management, the Logisticians are in charge of a business or organization’s supply chain, so they generally work in supply chain management (SCM).“http://www.bls.gov/ooh/business-and-financial/logisticians.html” ob duties include developing relationships with suppliers, ensuring all materials are transported in a timely manner, understanding customer needs, finding ways to minimize the cost of moving goods and many more.

Eligibility

  • Diploma
  • Undergraduate degree
  • Postgraduate degree
  • Postgraduate diploma level

One can opt for the part-time/full-time course as per their convenience. Each course has different eligibility criteria. One who has completed 10+2 is eligible for the undergraduate course (BBA). In the case of a Postgraduate degree, he/she should have at least 50% graduation marks from any recognized institution are eligible for a postgraduate degree, advanced certificate, and postgraduate diploma. Some universities conduct entrance examination for admission.

Scope

The scope of this course is very wide. It is a huge field with huge opportunities for talented managers in India as well as abroad. The market size of the logistics sector in India is high and has a wide opportunity. Sources estimate that the industry employs over 45 million people and is going at a rate of 15% per annum. You can work anywhere, whether government or private, as wholesalers or retailers. You can certify as Logistics Practitioner, Business Logistics, SCM and Transport Management, Warehouse supervisor, Shipping coordinator, Operations manager, Export executives, Expeditor, Logistics coordinator.

Skills that are associated with higher pay for this job are Inventory Management, Supply Chain, and Logistics. If you are well trained and experienced you can find employment abroad and get a good pay scale. If you are studying this course in a reputed institution, you can get a place in reputed companies and can get a good ambiance to work and get a good salary package.
