BSc Mathematics

Mathematics is the study of quantity, structure, space, and change. B.Sc. Mathematics or Bachelor of Science in Mathematics is a three-year undergraduate Mathematics course that provides a wide knowledge of mathematics. Mathematicians seek out patterns and formulate new conjectures. This course is a career-oriented course in nature. This degree covers some of the major topics that we have known some at the basic level from one's childhood.

Subjects offered in this course include Algebra, Integral Calculus & Trigonometry, Vector Analysis & Geometry, Different Equations, Mechanics, Mathematical Methods, Numerical Analysis, Differential Geometry, and Advanced Calculus, etc.. Mathematical equations can be applied directly to build applications in coding and cryptology, mathematical physics, mathematical biology, Bioinformatics, and finance.

Mathematics is the basis of life. Everything in our life is related to mathematical calculations. The scope of this course doesn't diminish. One who has opted for B.Sc. Mathematics can opt for several other courses such as MBA, etc. Or can even join in the Engineering discipline upon completion.

Eligibility

  • Candidates who have completed their 10+2 in Science stream with Mathematics as one of the subjects and 10+2 passed with at least 50% marks from a recognized board are eligible for this course.
  • The admission procedure may vary from college to college.
  • Candidates can get direct admission or entrance test depending upon the admission procedure

Scope

This course gives a good base for the applicants to go for higher degree programs in the respective subjects. They can become a private teacher in schools/colleges and can run their own coaching centers. They can also go for banking services both on the private and government sector such as bank P.O.s. Graduates have also job scopes in financial analysis, medical research, biotechnology, weather, and forecasting, etc. After doing B.Ed., candidates can become a government teacher or lecturer in schools or colleges.

The demand for mathematics graduates is very high in overseas countries. They are appointed as Teacher, Researcher, Statistician, Treasury Management Specialist, Demographer, and Economist, etc.
